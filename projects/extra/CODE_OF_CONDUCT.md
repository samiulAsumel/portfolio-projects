### Harassment and Discrimination

#### **âŒ Harassment Includes**

- Offensive comments related to gender, sexual orientation, disability, ethnicity, religion, or age
- Personal attacks, insults, or demeaning comments
- Intimidation, threats, or bullying
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Stalking or following someone online or offline
- Publishing private information without consent

#### **âŒ Discrimination Includes**

- Excluding someone based on protected characteristics
- Making assumptions about someone's abilities or interests
- Using stereotypes or generalizations
- Treating someone differently based on their background
- Creating a hostile or unwelcoming environment

### Unprofessional Conduct

#### **âŒ Professional Misconduct**

- Plagiarism or intellectual property theft
- Misrepresentation of credentials or experience
- Deliberate introduction of malicious code

- Violating privacy or confidentiality agreements

- Posting irrelevant or inappropriate content

- Engaging in flame wars or arguments

#### **âŒ Security Violations**

- Attempting to gain unauthorized access

- Sharing sensitive or confidential information
- Introducing backdoors or malicious code
- Violating security policies and procedures

### Communication Violations

#### **âŒ Poor Communication**

- Using excessive profanity or offensive language
- Making threats or violent statements
- Sharing inappropriate or explicit content
- Engaging in hate speech or extremist content
- Spreading misinformation or false information

### Enforcement Principles

#### **ðŸ” Investigation Process**

1. **Report Received** - Initial review and validation
2. **Evidence Collection** - Gather relevant information and context
3. **Assessment** - Evaluate against code of conduct standards
4. **Decision** - Determine appropriate action and response
5. **Communication** - Inform relevant parties of outcomes
6. **Documentation** - Record incident and resolution

#### **âš–ï¸ Enforcement Actions**

| Severity                 | Action                        | Duration   |
| ------------------------ | ----------------------------- | ---------- |
| **Warning**              | Private warning and education | Immediate  |
| **Temporary Suspension** | Restricted access             | 1-7 days   |
| **Temporary Ban**        | Full access restriction       | 1-4 weeks  |
| **Permanent Ban**        | Permanent exclusion           | Indefinite |

#### **ðŸŽ¯ Enforcement Criteria**

- **Intent** - Was the behavior intentional or accidental?
- **Impact** - What was the effect on others and the community?
- **History** - Is this a first offense or repeated behavior?
- **Context** - What were the circumstances and contributing factors?
- **Remediation** - Has the person taken responsibility and made amends?
